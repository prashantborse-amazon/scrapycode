About us

Global Sports Commerce (GSC) is one of the world’s largest Sports Technology and Management Companies providing dynamic Solutions, Sponsorship & Commercial Management, and Premier Consulting Services to leading global sports stakeholders – including sports bodies, leagues, rights holders, stadiums and brands. GSC, headquartered in Singapore, consists of 13 subsidiary companies with offices in 16 cities, across 10 countries.

Vision

Conceptualize, develop and promote next-generation technologies, that explore and deliver innovative commercial solutions for global sports stakeholders

Opportunity

Through cutting-edge technology solutions and meticulous fan data science, transform the industry from serving “programmatic” to “programmable” bespoke content thus helping Stakeholders derive more value from relationship with their fans
