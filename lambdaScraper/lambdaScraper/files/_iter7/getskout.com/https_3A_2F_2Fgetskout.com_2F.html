Cybersecurity for MSPs

24x7 Support

Dashboard View

Extension of Your Team

SKOUT's flexible alerting and support functions as an extension of your team by working directly with your NOC, help-desk, and technicians.

Fully-Managed

SKOUT connects the dots when it comes to cybersecurity. Layer in fully-managed security monitoring (SOC-as-a-Service), endpoint protection, and email protection and save on the hidden costs of configuration and management.

One Place for Security

Flexible Sensors

Write-Once, Deploy Anywhere technology allows us to connect logs from all devices, software, IaaS, and PaaS. Countless integrations molds to any technology stack.

Cloud-based AI Analytics

Continuously monitors and analyzes security information via multiple technologies and tools that track down potential threats and anomalies in real-time as they emerge.

Technology Augmentation

Everyone is aware of the cybersecurity talent shortage. By using AI and Machine Learning, SKOUT augments human capabilities, allowing for lower overhead and faster scaling.

System Error

Sometimes, an anomaly is just that. An outlier in a larger pattern doesn’t always indicate a larger attack. It takes a human to know the difference between danger and random data. Our team of security professionals is trained to know the difference.

Research Driven

With our analytics platform, SKOUT keeps ahead of the latest threats. We are constantly building and testing new defense models for our customers.
