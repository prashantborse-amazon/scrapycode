PEACOC™

High Density Fiber Management Platform

Go!Foton is proud to offer a remarkably new and innovative optical fiber management platform for Telecom and Datacenter applications which require high port capacity and density.

  • Up to 144 LC simplex or duplex connectors per 1RU
  • Unparalleled accessibility – easy, fast, and accurate
  • Craft friendly design for even “large hand” technicians
  • Access to both front & rear connectors at the same time
  • Configurable for numerous optical networking applications
