    About OneHub

    Transform your employee experience with OneHub…

    Meet OneHub

    It’s the home for all your people technology, combining all-things reward and benefits, recognition, and communications.

    • Design and deliver exceptional employee experiences
    • Intuitive, mobile-first technology
    • Fully integrated with your current HR systems
    • Real-time data insight and reporting
    Find out more Book a demo

    Benefits

    OneHub helps global organisations to run all their employee benefits in one place. It’s easy, intuitive, and works on any device, any time, anywhere.

    • Marketplace: a huge range of off-the-shelf benefits
    • Tailored benefits to suit you and your people
    • Broking and consulting services
    • Track and measure the impact of your scheme
    Find out more

    Recognition

    Our handy social recognition app means you and your people can say a meaningful, in-the-moment ‘thank you’, wherever you are, whatever you’re doing.

    • Accessible from any mobile, tablet or desktop
    • Acknowledge colleagues using GIFs, text, videos and memes
    • Real-time analytics help you measure engagement
    • Links seamlessly with OneHub | Reward
    Find out more

    Reward

    Works alongside OneHub | Recognition to add an extra bonus to your ‘thank you’ message. Reward your hard-working people with an instantly-redeemable voucher.

    • You have complete control over how your budget is assigned 
    • Easily track, amend, and move budget around
    • Instant, in-the-moment rewards for your people
    • Recipients can spend their reward with hundreds of retailers!
    Find out more

    Communications

    Communications Manager makes it easy to measure when, where and how often your employees engage with your internal communications.

    • Create and send beautiful branded content
    • Segment your audience for personalised, relevant messages
    • Fully integrated with your other HR systems
    • Measure the success of your emails and campaigns
    Find out more
