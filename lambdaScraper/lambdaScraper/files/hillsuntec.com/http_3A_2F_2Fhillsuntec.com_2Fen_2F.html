    ABOUT

    Guangzhou HillSun technology co., LTD founded in 2013, with 'technological innovation, product innovation, service platform' for the idea, to innovation and development of image data management technology, huge amounts of data intelligent analysis and with the mission of application service to continue to improve the user experience of industry application.

    HillSun as a national high-tech enterprise, focusing on in the field of artificial intelligence on the basis of the deep learning computer vision and pattern recognition, image structure, image data in areas such as the core technology breakthrough and development. And on the basis of core technologies for different industries to provide more data fusion and mining, and intelligent analysis application for data integrated management of cloud platform advantage. It solve industry users in image intelligent processing, image analysis, image portrait recognition, license plate recognition, data structured storage, intelligent retrieval, data analysis, plan execution and drills, emergency command, etc. The industry deep under the support of the industry demand of data management applications.

    HillSun has a number of independent intellectual property and high-tech products. And established technical support teams and service centers in more than 20 states and cities in China, Southeast Asia, Australia and the United States, so as to provide fast and high-quality service for customer.

    Guangzhou HillSun technology co., LTD is committed to becoming 'pioneer of image data management and application technology' in China.
