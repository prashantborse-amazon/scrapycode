Mobile Backhaul

Mobile Backhaul is an affordable and seamless path to high performance Ethernet WAN and wireless 4G
mobile backhaul services

The HFR HA-800 series is a cost-effective, multiservice, carrier-grade tabletop or wall-wall-mount compact unit
designed to deliver Ethernet services in support of enterprise business and wireless mobile backhaul
service applications. Its small form factor, low-power, temperature-resilient, no-fan design makes
the HA-800 series a good choice for installation at wireless towers (indoor/outdoor-ready) or customer
premises, solving space and power challenges, delivering high performance and scalable services, and
reducing operational costs.

Standards-Based Multiservice Delivery
Designed to support Metro Ethernet Forum (MEF) specifications, the HA-800 series delivers Ethernet
Private Line, Ethernet Virtual Private Line, Ethernet LAN, Ethernet Access, and Ethernet Tree based
connectivity in P2P, P2MP, and ring topologies for high capacity business and mobile backhaul 4G services.

Service Level Provisioning, Monitoring and Management
Graphical user interfaces based upon SNMP supported by in-band or out-of-band interface options are
available to provide users a full solutions management view: the individual node; the end-to-end
network; and the deployed services. With dedicated hardware for traffic performance monitoring, the
HA-800 series delivers on today's requirement for hardware based Y.1731 frame delay (FD), delay variation
(IFDV), and frame loss ratio (FLR) measurements. Tiered Ethernet services as well as wireless backhaul
service level performance may also be monitored through the web-based SLA portal.

Flexible and Reliable Carrier Ethernet Service
The HA-805 Ethernet Service Access device is architected in a small portable form -factor with SFP/SFP+ port
flexibility, providing Ethernet services demarcation, classification, management, prioritization,
synchronization, and service inter-working in up to 40 Gbps switching fabrics. High reliability is provided
for Ethernet services with Ethernet ring protection (G.8032v2).

  • Compact, low-power, and temperature-resilient unit ready for tower or CPE deployment, delivering high
    performance and reducing operational cost
  • Standards-based MEF Carrier Ethernet 2.0 Certification
  • Broad Ethernet services support provides affordable and seamless path to high-capacity business
    Ethernet and wireless 4G services
  • Synchronization source derived from Synchronous Ethernet interfaces or IEEE 1588v2 Ordinary Clock (Slave Clock)
  • System clock meets the accuracy, stability, and holdover specifications required for stratum
    3 Timing (G.813)
  • Carrier-grade platform with low latency and highly reliable service delivery with sub-50ms Ethernet
    Ring Protection (G.8032v2)
  • Deep Fault and Performance Management with Y.1731, embedded RFC2544, Y.1564 SAT and CIR/EIR
    utilization per EVC/UNI accessible through the Customer SLA Portal
